LOGO DESIGNING

Unleash your creativity and craft an innovative logo based on a surprise theme revealed at the event.

Event Rules

1. Participants must design a logo digitally using software like canva.

2. The theme will be provided on the spot.

3. If you want to work with Adobe tools like Photoshop and illustrator you are advised to bring your laptops.

4. Time limit: 1 hour.

5. Submission format: JPEG and PNG, along with a concept note.

6. Participants would be judged based on originality, theme relevance and aesthetic appeal.

7. The decision of the judge is final.

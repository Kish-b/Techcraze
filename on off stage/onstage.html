General Rules

1. Treat others with kindness, dignity, and empathy, regardless of differences in race, gender, culture, or beliefs.

2. Be Honest - Truthfulness fosters trust and integrity in relationships, whether personal, professional, or societal.

3. Take Responsibility - Own your actions and their consequences. Accountability is key to personal growth and societal well-being.

4. Practice Empathy - Try to understand and share the feelings of others. Empathy helps build stronger, more compassionate relationships.

5. Be Compassionate - Show kindness and concern for others' well-being, especially in times of need or distress.

6. Learn and Grow - Embrace lifelong learning, self-improvement, and adaptability. Growth is essential to becoming a better person and contributing to society.

7. Be Open-minded - Be willing to listen, learn, and consider other perspectives. Openness fosters understanding and progress.
